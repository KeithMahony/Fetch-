Who are we?

We are a group of third year students in the National College of Ireland, studying Computing. We are working on developing an android application as part of our Team Project module.

The group consists of four members; Matthew Byrne, Keith Mahony, Matthew Kearns, and Ben Carroll.
Check back in soon for more updates on the app!

Future Prospects

In the future, we plan to release a bracelet like tracking product as a companion to the app that will allow parents to know their children's whereabouts at all times and have a 'panic' button for the children to press in case of emergency. The application will also featuere a behaviour log, alerting parents or guardians to any unusual behaviour or activity exhibited in their child.
The bracelet, coupled with the app will of course incorporate all features present in the proof of concept version.
For the moment, we are using the capabilities of android phones to act as the bracelet, as the proof of concept.
