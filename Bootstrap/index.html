Fetch!
Android application

Our ‘bracelet’ like product will track a user's child, providing real-time location information. The app will monitor the child's behaviour, searching for unusual activity (increased heart rate, movement speed, being outside ‘safety net’ area, and more), and notifying the app user of it via push notification. However, the child can trigger this warning manually by pressing the ‘panic button’, found on their bracelet. The user can also view statistical data collected by the bracelet, allowing them to monitor things such as heart rate averages, ensuring their child's healthy lifestyle.
